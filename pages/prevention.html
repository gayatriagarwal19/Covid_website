COVID-19 Prevention

Prevention Overview

Preventing the spread of COVID-19 is essential to protect yourself and others. Here are some key prevention measures:

  • Wear a mask in public places.
  • Maintain at least 6 feet of physical distance from others.
  • Wash your hands frequently with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.
  • Use hand sanitizer with at least 60% alcohol if soap and water are not available.

Wearing Masks

Properly wearing a mask can help reduce the spread of the virus. Here's how:

  1. Ensure the mask covers your nose and mouth completely.
  2. Avoid touching the mask while wearing it.
  3. Wash reusable masks regularly.
  4. Follow local guidelines for mask use.

Social Distancing

Social distancing is important to minimize close contact with others. Follow these guidelines:

  • Avoid large gatherings and crowded places.
  • Stay at least 6 feet away from people who are not from your household.
  • Limit non-essential travel.

Hand Hygiene

Proper hand hygiene is crucial in preventing the spread of COVID-19:

  • Wash your hands often, especially after being in public places.
  • Use soap and water for at least 20 seconds.
  • If soap and water are not available, use hand sanitizer with at least 60% alcohol.
